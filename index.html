Dead Simple Blogging Engine For Rails Apps

Stop developing your own blogs and start spending time writing your actual content with Fuel. View the gem on Github.

gem install fuel

  • Download & install

    Add the gem to your Gemfile or download it from Github.

  • Create & Edit Content

    Login to your blog's admin panel without any additional setup and start creating new posts with one click.

  • Publish Content

    Select a publish date, an author and let Fuel take care of the rest.
